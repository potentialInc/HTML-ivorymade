How do I place a new order?
Navigate to Orders → New Order. Select your restoration type, upload your case files, and complete the order form. You'll receive a confirmation email with your order ID.
What file formats do you accept?
We accept STL, PLY, OBJ for digital scans, and JPEG, PNG for photos. Maximum file size is 50MB per file.
How long does production take?
Standard production time is 5-7 business days for most restorations. Complex cases or high-volume orders may take up to 10 business days.
What is your revision policy?
We offer one complimentary revision per case if requested within 14 days of delivery. Additional revisions may incur a processing fee.
How do I track my order?
You can track your order status in real-time through the Orders dashboard. Once shipped, a tracking number from our logistics partner will be provided.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), ACH transfers, and modern digital wallets including Apple Pay and Google Pay.
Can I cancel an order?
Orders can be cancelled for a full refund if the cancellation request is made before the case enters the 'Production' stage.
How does the referral program work?
Invite fellow practitioners via your unique link in the Referral tab. Once they complete their first case, both accounts receive lab credit.
