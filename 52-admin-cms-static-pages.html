Terms and Conditions

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using IVORYMADE's dental laboratory services, you agree to be bound by these terms and conditions. These terms govern the relationship between your dental practice and our laboratory facility regarding all custom dental restorations and services provided.

2. Services

IVORYMADE provides high-quality, custom dental restorations including crowns, bridges, veneers, and implant components. All orders are manufactured according to the digital or physical impressions provided. Standard turnaround times vary by product category and will be specified at the time of order placement.

3. Payment Terms

Accounts are eligible for net-30 payment terms upon approval. Statements are issued on the last business day of each month. Payments not received within 30 days of the invoice date may be subject to a late fee of 1.5% per month. IVORYMADE reserves the right to hold new cases for accounts with balances exceeding 60 days.
